## What "adapted" means here

Each adapted skill below was re-written to fit re-forge's conventions while
preserving the substantive methodology, checklists, and workflows that make the
original valuable. Specifically, we:

- replaced ECC's frontmatter with re-forge's `name` + `description` format;
- rebranded product/path references to re-forge (e.g. `~/.claude/...`);
- inlined the essential logic of any ECC-only helper scripts/tools as prose so
  each skill is self-contained with no broken references;
- condensed and re-focused content for readability.

These files are derivative adaptations, not verbatim reproductions.
